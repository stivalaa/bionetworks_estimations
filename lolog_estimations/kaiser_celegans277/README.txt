mac95.mat
mac95.zip	Macaque cortical connectivity network within one hemisphere. The files include the adjacency matrix, the labels of the cortical regions, and the spatial positions of the regions as three-dimensional coordinates (the unit is mm).
celegans131.mat
celegans131.zip	C. elegans local network of 131 frontal neurons. The files include the adjacency matrix, the labels of the neurons, and the spatial positions of the neurons as two-dimensional coordinates (unit is mm).
celegans277.mat
celegans277.zip	C. elegans global network of 277 neurons. The files include the adjacency matrix, the labels of the neurons, and the spatial positions of the neurons as two-dimensional coordinates (unit is mm).
